Welcome to Fast Record!

This privacy policy explains how Fast Record collects, uses and stores your personal information and what your rights are. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions about the content of this privacy policy, you can contact us using the contact details provided in this document.

It is important that you read and fully understand this Privacy Policy before using the software and related services we provide, and use/continue to use Fast Record after you have agreed to it in its entirety. By using our products and related services, you are consenting to our collection, use and storage of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. However, this does not mean that we collect, use and store your personal information as defined in this Privacy Policy whenever you start using our products and related services. We will only process your personal information for the purposes of providing you with our services on a “minimum necessary” basis when you use certain features.

We take the protection of your personal information very seriously and will take appropriate security measures to protect the security of your personal information in accordance with national laws and generally applicable security standards in the industry. If you would like further and more detailed information, please read the appropriate section according to the following index.

I. How we collect, use and store your personal information

(i) The personal information we collect and use

We collect and use your personal information to carry out the following functions.

\1. to ensure that the basic functions of Fast Record are implemented properly and to provide you with a quality service

In the course of your use of the products and related services provided by us, we may collect the following information from you, as appropriate, in order to implement the basic functions of the products and to optimise the experience of using them.

·Device information: hardware model, operating system version number, International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI), network device hardware address (MAC), IP address, software version number, network access method and type. Please understand that this information is essential for us to collect in order to provide our services and ensure the proper functioning of our products.

·Use information: operation logs, crash reports and other information

\2. Information uploaded or provided during your use of Fast Record

When you use the Fast Record feedback service, we will collect the contact information you provide when you provide feedback, as appropriate. We will use this contact information to provide you with feedback, necessary announcements and notifications (which are not advertisements), and for anti-fraud purposes; if we use this information to push advertisements to you, you have the right to refuse and we will stop pushing them to you at your request.

\3. to provide you with the core functions of recording video

Screen Recordering, Facecam Recording, Reaction Recording and Live Recording are core features of Fast Record. In order to implement such features, we will request permission for camera, album and microphone permissions when you trigger use, in order to enable the recording of your screen, camera and microphone content and saving it to your local album. If you refuse to grant the necessary permissions required to provide the core functionality described above, you will not be able to use the relevant functionality, but it will not affect your ability to use the other features of Fast Record as normal.

\4. to provide you with the core functions of editing video

Video editing is a core feature of Fast Record. To enable this functionality, we will request permissions for albums, media libraries and microphones when you trigger use, in order to enable you to select local album files or recorded screen files within Fast Record for video editing, add media library music, add audio recordings, add subtitles and other features. If you refuse to authorise the necessary permissions required to provide the core functions listed above, you will not be able to use the relevant functions, but this will not affect your normal use of the other functions of Fast Record.

For details of the permissions that need to be requested and used in the use of the core functions of Fast Record, please refer to the Application and Use of Permissions attached at the end of this document.

\5. We may also collect various other information from you in the course of our services that you have volunteered, depending on the specific features you use and the services you have requested from us. Please note that any other information we collect based on your unsolicited behaviour will only be used for the purposes we have explicitly stated.

\6. In addition, in accordance with relevant laws, regulations and national standards, we do not require your authorised consent to collect and use your personal information in the following circumstances.

6.1. in connection with the fulfilment of the obligations imposed by law and regulations on the controller of personal data.

6.2. directly related to national security and defence security.

6.3. which are directly related to public safety, public health, or significant public interest.

6.4. directly related to criminal investigations, prosecutions, trials and enforcement of sentences.

6.5. where it is difficult to obtain the authorised consent of the person for the protection of the life, property or other significant legitimate interests of the subject of the personal data or other individuals.

6.6. when the personal information in question is disclosed to the public by the subject of the personal information himself/herself.

6.7. necessary for the conclusion and performance of a contract at the request of the subject of the personal data.

6.8. where personal information is collected from legitimate publicly disclosed information, such as legitimate news reports, government information disclosure and other sources.

6.9. necessary to maintain the safe and stable operation of the product or service provided, such as detecting and dealing with faults in the product or service.

6.10. where the controller of the personal information is a news outlet and it is necessary for it to carry out legitimate news reporting.

6.11. where the controller of personal information is an academic research institution, where it is necessary to carry out statistical or academic research in the public interest, and where it provides the results of academic research or descriptions to the public, and where it de-identifies the personal information contained in the results.

(ii) Storage of information

\1. Where information is stored

Personal information collected from users will be stored within China or in the user’s country of residence in accordance with laws and regulations.

\2. Duration of information storage

Sharing with explicit consent: With your explicit consent, we will only retain your personal information, such as the email address you provide in your feedback, for as long as is necessary to achieve the purpose. When you have not logged in to Fast Record for more than 180 days, we will delete the corresponding information data.

In the event that our products or services are discontinued, we will notify you by push notification, announcement, etc. and delete or anonymise your personal information within a reasonable period of time.

II. How we share, transfer and publicly disclose your personal information

At present, we do not actively share or transfer your personal information to any other company, organisation or individual. In the event of any other sharing, transfer or public disclosure of your personal information, we will inform you of the purpose of the processing and obtain your prior express consent.

\2. For the purposes stated in this policy, certain of our services will be entrusted to authorised partners (with whom we will enter into relevant processing agreements and monitor their personal data processing activities in accordance with legal requirements).

2.1. advertising services partners

2.2. third party statistical and analytical services partners

We will strictly and carefully assess the purposes for which third parties use the shared information, conduct a comprehensive assessment of the security and safety capabilities of these partners (e.g. strict security monitoring of the software tool development packages and application program interfaces from which the information is obtained) and require them to comply with the legal agreements for cooperation. At the same time, we will only share your personal information that is necessary to provide our services for legitimate, necessary and explicit purposes.

For more information on the SDK of our access partners, please refer to the Catalogue of Access to Third Party SDK attached at the end of this document.

\3. In addition, in accordance with relevant laws, regulations and national standards, we may share, transfer and publicly disclose your personal information without your prior authorisation in the following circumstances.

3.1. in connection with the performance of our obligations under laws and regulations.

3.2. directly related to national security and defence security.

3.3. which are directly related to public safety, public health, or significant public interest.

3.4. directly related to criminal investigations, prosecutions, trials and enforcement of sentences.

3.5. where it is necessary to protect the life, property or other significant legitimate interests of the subject of the personal information or other individuals but it is difficult to obtain their authorised consent.

3.6. personal information that the subject of the personal information wants the public to disclose on his own.

3.7. where personal information is collected from legitimate publicly disclosed information, such as legitimate news reports, government information disclosure and other sources.

3.8. other circumstances as provided for by laws and administrative regulations.

III. How we protect the security of your personal information

\1. We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect the personal information you provide to us from unauthorised access, public disclosure, use, modification, damage or loss. For example, we will use reasonably practicable means such as encryption technology that is no less than industry standard, anonymisation, etc. to process your personal information.

\2. At the same time, we will establish corresponding sound security protection mechanism, security management system, data security process, special security department to protect your personal information.

Nevertheless, the Internet environment is not 100% secure. Please be aware and understand that despite the reasonable and effective security measures described above, we cannot always guarantee the security of information due to technical limitations, possible unknown malicious means and the systems and communication networks you use when using our products and accessing our services. In the event of a breach of user information security, we will promptly inform you of the basic situation and possible impact of the security incident, the measures we have taken or will take to deal with it, suggestions for you to prevent and mitigate the risk on your own, and remedial measures for you, in accordance with legal and regulatory requirements. We will promptly inform you of the incident in the form of push notifications, emails, letters, SMS, etc. When it is difficult to inform you individually, we will take reasonable and effective ways to make announcements. At the same time, we will also report on the disposal of user information security incidents in accordance with the requirements of relevant regulatory authorities.

\4. We would like to remind you that the personal information protection measures provided in this Privacy Policy apply only to the Fast Record software and related services. Once you leave Fast Record and related services to browse or use other websites, services and content resources, we will have no ability or obligation to protect any personal information submitted by you on software or websites outside of the Fast Record software and related services, whether or not your access to, browsing of, or use of such software or websites is based on a link to or from Fast Record links or referrals.

IV. Your rights

We take the management of your personal information very seriously and make every effort to safeguard your rights to access, correct/amend, delete, withdraw authorization to complain and other privacy controls over your personal information so that you have the ability to safeguard your privacy and the security of your personal information.

\1. withdrawal of system-sensitive authorizations

You can change the authorised permissions in the system settings. If you decide to withdraw your authorisation, we will no longer collect information relating to these permissions, but at the same time this will result in the relevant functions no longer being available on . At the same time, your decision to change or withdraw your authorisation will not affect the processing of personal data that we have previously carried out on the basis of your authorisation.

\2. Delete

You can delete your personal files and other data generated in Fast Record

\3. the right to inform you of the cessation of operations

If we cease operations, we will cease collecting your personal information in a timely manner, notify you of the cessation of operations by delivering notice on a case-by-case basis or by announcement, and delete or anonymise any personal information held about you.

V. How we handle the personal information of minors

We take the protection of personal information of minors very seriously. However, due to the online environment of the Internet, it is not possible for us to verify the true identity of each user.

If you are a minor, we ask that you ask your parents or other guardians to read this privacy policy carefully and to use the Fast Record product and related services with their consent, in accordance with the relevant legal requirements.

If you are the parent or other guardian of a minor, please be aware that you have read this Privacy Policy carefully and used it with the minor in your custody before using the Fast Record product and related services. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a minor in your custody without your prior consent, we will seek to delete it as soon as possible. If you have questions about the content of personal information relating to a minor in your custody, please contact us using the contact details we have provided.

VI. How this policy is updated

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time in response to changes in business, laws, regulations or policies. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your express consent. When this Privacy Policy is updated, we will issue an updated version and alert you to the updated terms by notice, posting or other appropriate means before the updated terms take effect so that you are kept up to date with the latest version of this Policy. We will only collect, store and use your personal information in accordance with the updated Privacy Policy if we have obtained your express consent to do so. If you do not agree to the revised Privacy Policy, you have the right to and should immediately stop using Fast Record products and related services. If you have any questions about this updated Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us using the contact information we have provided.

VII. How you can contact us

If you have any questions, comments, suggestions or complaints about the content of this privacy policy, you can contact us at the following email address: abeyondfor@126.com. We will review the issue as soon as possible and respond within fifteen working days of verifying your user identity.
